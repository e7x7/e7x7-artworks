Contemporary Painting

Contemporary painting is characterized by its great diversity: artists explore the boundaries of traditional painting by trying to exceed them, by using new mediums combined with paint in their paintings such as ink, sand, glass and even cement. Contemporary painting often oscillates between abstract painting and figurative painting
